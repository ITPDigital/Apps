Modest Fashion Weekend is almost here, and we’ve got exciting details about our fabulous fashion line-up. Kicking the weekend off from 4pm on Thursday March 7, brands including Promod, Stradivarius, Next and Topshop will take to the catwalk, followed by Marks & Spencer, Maje, Sandro and Massimo Dutti on Friday.

Are you ready?

As well as showcases from some of our favourite brands, Friday’s activities will include a make-up masterclass with celebrity make-up artist Soraya Shawky and TV presenter Mays Anber at 5pm. House of Fraser, Aspinal of London and more will be showcasing their collections on Saturday to round off the weekend (as if we didn’t spoil you enough), before Grazia’s Editor in Chief Alison Tay takes to the stage for an International Women’s Day panel discussion alongside Dima Ayad, Grazia’s advocate for body positivity, and Nayla Al Khaja, Grazia Girl Gang ambassador for film. You won’t want to miss this conversation with the Grazia Girl Gang (they’re pretty great, if you ask us), as they’ll be discussing blazing a trail and facing fears - so make sure to tear yourself away from the henna artist by 5.30pm.

Marks & Spencer is just one of the brands joining us for Modest Fashion Weekend

We’re sure you don’t need reminding, but don’t forget to visit the pop-up shops between shows! Phew, we think it’s time for a lie down.
